Acousto Electric Amplifier on LNOI Platform

This project explores acousto-electric amplification on the lithium niobate on insulator platform, with particular interest in the coupling between acoustic waves, electric fields, and guided device behavior on an integrated substrate.

The work aims to clarify the physical conditions under which amplification effects may be observed or enhanced, while also informing the design of compact acoustic or electro-acoustic components on LNOI.

Project Scope

The project includes analysis of coupling mechanisms, device geometry considerations, and the relationship between platform properties and measurable amplification-related effects.

Current Focus

  • Understanding the physical mechanism behind gain behavior.
  • Evaluating how the LNOI platform supports integrated implementation.
  • Identifying device configurations suitable for experimental study.

Expected Outcome

The expected outcome is a clearer framework for designing and interpreting acousto-electric amplification experiments on LNOI, with potential value for integrated acoustic signal processing.
